		<div class="col-md-8">
			<p>
				The Ready Steady Pan Plugin is recommended for better panning experience, but is not required.
			</p>
			<p>
				A plugin made by <a href="https://forums.alliedmods.net/member.php?u=38829" target="_blank">pheadxdll</a> and updated by 200 restricts all players to only use non-combat and melee weapons.
			</p>
			<p>
				We strongly suggest you to make sure you are using the settings below. If you use our config (which is required), you should have them executed automatically.				
			</p>			
			<p><code>
				melee 1<br>
				melee_healing 0<br>
				melee_jumper 1<br>
				melee_jetpack 1<br>
				melee_circuit 0<br>
				melee_flag z<br>
				melee_voteflag z<br>
				melee_disabled 0<br>
				melee_arena 0<br>
				melee_mode 0<br>		
			</code></p>
