                <h2 class="text-center">Who are we?</h2>
                <p>A lot of people put work into making Ready Steady Pan a reality. Season 3 almost didn't happen; only after a three year hiatus was it brought back to life.</p>
                <p>Ready Steady Pan was created by smitzcraft and run by a small team of volunteers. It gained a lot of popular interest worldwide, spurring head admin paced to administer the second season in 2013.</p>
                <p>An attempt to start up Season 3 went ongoing for three years. Many of our staff left, then came back as their lives got more and less busy. No matter what happened, though, we came back to Ready Steady Pan. We are the Ready Steady Pan Team!</p>
